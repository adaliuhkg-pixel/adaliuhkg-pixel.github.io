Explore essential vocabulary categories to enhance your Japanese language skills.

Greetings

こんにちは (Konnichiwa) - Hello

さようなら (Sayōnara) - Goodbye

Numbers

一 (Ichi) - One

二 (Ni) - Two

三 (San) - Three

Food

ご飯 (Gohan) - Rice/Meal

肉 (Niku) - Meat

魚 (Sakana) - Fish

Common Phrases

ありがとう (Arigatou) - Thank you

すみません (Sumimasen) - Excuse me/Sorry
